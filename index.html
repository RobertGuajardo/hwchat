Platform Capabilities

Built for Hillwood. Powered by AI.

Everything Copilot Studio can't do — and more.

🏠
Live Property Search
The chatbot queries the Cecilian XO API in real time — surfacing available homes, pricing, builders, and move-in dates during the conversation.
🧠
Semantic Knowledge Base
pgvector-powered search that understands meaning, not just keywords. Scrape a community website and the bot learns everything on it.
📊
Lead Capture to HubSpot
Leads are captured naturally within the conversation flow, enriched with context, scored for quality, and routed to HubSpot automatically.
🎨
Per-Community Branding
Each community gets its own colors, logo, greeting, quick replies, and AI personality — all configured from the dashboard.
🔗
Cross-Community Intelligence
When a visitor's needs don't match, the bot can recommend sibling communities. No Hillwood lead is lost to a poor match.
🌐
Bilingual Ready
The AI handles Spanish natively. Add Spanish KB entries for key communities and the bot switches seamlessly.
